Final Project

I’m seriously a candy lover, especially the sweet and sour ones. Gummy candies are my all-time favorite—chewy, fruity, and just so fun to eat without getting bored. And don’t even get me started on hard candy! It’s a classic, perfect for slowly enjoying the flavor as it melts in your mouth, making every bite last longer.

I also have a soft spot for candies with gooey fillings like caramel or chocolate. There’s just something so satisfying about biting into one and feeling the filling melt in your mouth. On days when I need a little sweetness to boost my mood, just one piece of candy can do the trick. Honestly, candy isn’t just a snack—it’s a little dose of happiness!
